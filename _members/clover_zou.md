Clover is an NYU undergraduate majoring in Psychology on the cognition and neuroscience track. Her current research focuses on how schemata shape memory representations during encoding and retrieval. She is also interested in neuro-methodology, particularly in using EEG to examine brain electrical activity and the temporal dynamics underlying memory processes. She is currently assisting with ongoing EEG and behavioral studies in the lab.
